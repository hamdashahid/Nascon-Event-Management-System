Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in NASCON?

NASCON is open to all university students across the country. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria, which will be mentioned in the event details.

How do I register for events?

You can register online by creating an account and signing up for your preferred events through the dashboard after logging in.

Are there accommodation facilities?

Yes, participants traveling from other cities can request accommodation during registration. Rooms are allocated based on availability and preferences.

How can my company become a sponsor?

Visit the Sponsorships section or contact us directly to explore partnership opportunities and sponsorship packages.
